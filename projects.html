Energy Analyses "Landhotel Hirschen"

Led a comprehensive energy analysis for a rural hotel, focusing on system optimization and sustainability potential. Assessed and proposed improvements for lighting, cooling systems (Kältemittel), and the expansion of photovoltaic installations. Used tools like DialuxEvo, PV*Sol, and Polysun to simulate performance, analyze energy flows, and develop future-ready solutions. Contributed both technically and strategically — from simulations to presenting feasible upgrade paths.
